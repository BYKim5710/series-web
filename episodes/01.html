Episode overview

This episode introduces the author and outlines the core direction of the NCG Religious Revolution Series—a project that challenges institutional distortions of the sages’ teachings and proposes a new framework grounded in consilience.

Core theme: restoring truth by reading, comparing, and verifying—rather than inheriting unexamined belief.

What you’ll find in Episode 01

  • Who the author is, and how the project began
  • Why “consilience” matters for religion, ethics, and truth
  • The book framework behind the series (including Conversation with Oneness God)
